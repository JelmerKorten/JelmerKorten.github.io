Banana Pancakes


enjoy this delicious breakfast

Ingredients:

  • 0.5 cup of oats
  • 0.5 cup of flour
  • 1 egg
  • 0.5 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of cinnamon
  • 1 banana
  • olive oil (for frying)
  • pinch of salt
  • some water read method below
Method:
  1. in a bowl, mix all ingredients besides the water, oil and banana
  2. mix in a little bit of water to get a paste
  3. you don't want to add too much water so go slowly, this goes on feeling
  4. the amount of water needed depends on size of egg and flour/oats used
  5. the mixture will be quite thin, with some bits in it (the oats)
  6. peel the banana and slice thin slices above the bowl so it falls straight in
  7. mix carefully to not break the banana
  8. heat some olive oil in a frying pan and make sure it hot
  9. add the mixture
  10. after the initial searing of the mixture turn the heat down a bit
  11. when small bubbles form in the pan and the top is not pure mixture anymore it is time for a flip
  12. after flipping fry it another 2-3 minutes on medium heat until the pancake moves when you shake the pan
  13. breakfast is served!
Comments / Ideas:
  • if you're making multiple pancakes you can expect to add some water for the 2nd one as the oats absorb the water
  • make sure the oil is hot, the seared edges give it a nice extra crunch
  • there's no sugar in the mixture but the banana will add some sweetness
  • add extra honey or syrup of your liking if you want
  • try with some peanut butter or nutella on top
