Quinoa Chocolate Bars


enjoy this delicious snack

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw Quinoa
  • 1 cup chocolate to melt
  • 1-2 tablespoons coconut oil
Method:
  1. roast / puff the quinoa in a frying pan, stir a lot!
  2. at the same time: melt the chocolate + coconut oil
  3. when the quinoa is popped (like popcorn) take it off the heat
  4. mix the quinoa and chocolate together in bowl or pan
  5. add some parchment paper in a small flat container
  6. add the mixture to the container
  7. freeze for 1hr
  8. enjoy!
  9. for bonus: add some chocolate chips after mixing the quinoa in
