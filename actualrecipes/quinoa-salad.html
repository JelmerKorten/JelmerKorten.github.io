Quinoa Salad


enjoy this delicious lunch

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of uncooked quinoa
  • 1 cucumber
  • 0.5 cup parsley
  • 0.5 cup of mint
  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 0.5 cups of chopped pistachios (chopped)
  • 0.5 cups of feta (crumbled/chopped)
  • 0.5 small red onion (chopped)
  • juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • salt and pepper
Method:
  1. cook the quinoa
  2. chop everything small
  3. mix well and enjoy luke warm or cold
Comments / Ideas:
  • personally I do not like parsley and mint so I just leave them out, they will however make it more fresh if you don't mind them
  • it's a good fresh base salad
  • try adding other ingredients like avocado or (sundried) tomatoes
  • try swapping the feta for mozzerella
  • different nuts are an option too!
  • great for addition on a BBQ or as stand alone meal
