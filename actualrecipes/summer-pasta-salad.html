Summer Pasta Salad


enjoy this delicious snack

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of pasta
  • 1 cup of sweet corn
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 0.5 cups of sundried tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1-2 cups zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1 red onion (diced)
  • 0.75 cups of feta
  • olive oil (for frying)
  • salt and pepper
The Dressing:
  • 0.5 cups use a bit less! of olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons of white wine vinegar (or any other vinegar really)
  • 1 teaspoon of dried dill
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teapsoon of oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of (dijon) mustard
  • 0.5 teaspoon of garlic powder (can use fresh, just chop very small, or have some garlic oil ready)
Method:
  1. boil the pasta
  2. while that is happening:
  3. boil some water and cover the peas / corn if it was frozen (or microwave!)
  4. brown the onion, add in bell pepper and zucchini
  5. when pasta is done, strain it
  6. add it all together in pan or big bowl
  7. mix carefully to not break the feta
Comments / Ideas:
  • if you want it more visually appealling you could fry/grill everything separately and present it like a buddha bowl
  • this is a good base pasta salad you could easily add on to
  • some ideas to add onto this:
  • try with avocado and cucumber
  • try with more sundried tomatoes
  • try with tuna and tangy mayo (I would change the feta for something like mozzeralla)
