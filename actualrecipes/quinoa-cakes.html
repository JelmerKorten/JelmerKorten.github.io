Quinoa Cakes


enjoy this delicious snack

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups Quinoa
  • 1 sjallot / small onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 0.5 cups of grated parmesan
  • 0.25 cups parsley
  • 2 tablespoons oregano
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 4 large eggs
  • olive oil (for frying)
  • salt and pepper
Method:
  1. Cook and cool the quinoa
  2. prepare two plates
  3. Finely chop the sjallot, garlic, herbs and cheese
  4. When quinoa is cool, add the chopped ingredients
  5. add breadcrumbs and salt and pepper and stir
  6. beat the 4 eggs and mix them in
  7. stir untill evenly moistured
  8. pack the mixture tightly in a 1/4 cup measure and place on plate
  9. once you have 4-5 raw quinoa cakes on the plate you can alternate between frying / packing
  10. panfry the quinoa cakes in olive oil for about 5 mins per side or until golden brown and crispy
  11. let cool and serve
Comments / Ideas:
  • try with some avocado on top + lemon juice
  • maybe you could put some cling film into the 1/4 cup to help get it out? you'll notice it will be quite sticky
  • when packing the mixture into the shape, think: you're a kid in a candy shop and you can only bring as much as fit in the measure
  • try different cheeses and herbs!
